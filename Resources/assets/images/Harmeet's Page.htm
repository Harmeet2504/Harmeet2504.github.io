About Me

I completed PhD in January, 2019 in bioinformatics from UNESCO-Regional Centre for Biotechnology (RCB), Delhi-NCR, India.

One of my biggest achievements have been cracking three national fellowships (ICMR-JRF, CSIR-JRF, GATE) for pursuing doctorate. I was awarded with CSIR fellowship for my PhD to understand features and dynamics associated with immune response.

Due to a keen interest in data science and machine learning, I pursued certification from Rutgers University, New Jersey, February, 2020. Skills encompass developing solutions to a whole end-to-end data-modeling project-from data munging, defining relevant questions, pattern recognition, evaluate risks, implement tools and best practices to enhance decision-making and draw meaningful insights to communicate to relevant stakeholders.

A proponent of knowledge dissemination and experiential learning to peers.
