Harmeet Kaur, PhD

Data Scientist | Bioinformatician

Dr. Harmeet Kaur is a senior data scientist with a flair for transforming chaotic data into wisdom. Passionate about healthcare innovation, she currently harnesses LLMs at Johnson and Johnson to unlock critical factors influencing patient and provider decisions. Her journey includes designing novel therapeutics in Harris' lab at Genentech, California, where she explored antibody-antigen interactions, and creating APIs for a federated molecular resource with the late Prof. John Westbrook at RCSB Protein Data Bank. As a health data science fellow at Insight, Boston, she developed a machine learning app to predict gestational diabetes, driving timely interventions. With a strong foundation in computational biology, her PhD focused on how antibodies adapt structurally to various antigens. Dr. Kaur's achievements include prestigious fellowships from Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR), Council of Scientifc and Industrial Research (CSIR) and Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) that showcase her dedication to advancing healthcare solution through research and development.

She is a team player who promotes experiential learning among peers, advocating for psychological safety in the workplace. For any queries and consultations, shoot her an email at meet.academia@gmail.com.
